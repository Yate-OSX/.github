## 🖌 Overview  
Yate Advanced is a powerful audio tag editor built for music enthusiasts and professionals who demand complete control over the metadata in their music library. It handles every major audio format and goes far deeper than any basic tag editor.

Batch editing lets you apply changes to thousands of files at once using rule-based actions. Rename files from tags, fill missing metadata from templates, and standardize your entire library in a single automated pass.

Online database integration connects to Discogs, MusicBrainz, and other sources to look up accurate metadata for any release. Match your files to authoritative records and fill gaps automatically.

The scripting engine lets you build custom automation workflows for complex tagging tasks. Combine conditions, transformations, and lookups into repeatable scripts that handle the edge cases your library throws at you.

For audiophiles and music archivists who maintain large, carefully organized libraries, Yate Advanced is the metadata tool that makes every file exactly right.

## 🚀 Why Choose Yate Advanced?

Yate Advanced represents the perfect balance between powerful functionality and elegant simplicity. Built specifically for Apple Silicon, this application takes full advantage of cutting-edge technologies including Metal graphics acceleration, Grand Central Dispatch for multi-threading, and native components that ensure buttery-smooth performance even on older hardware.

The application has been meticulously optimized for both Intel-based systems and the latest Apple Silicon chips, including M1, M2, M3, and M4 processors. Whether you are running older hardware or the latest devices, you will experience the same level of responsiveness and efficiency.

What truly sets Yate Advanced apart is its commitment to user privacy and data security. All processing happens locally on your device, with no data ever sent to external servers. Your documents and work remain completely private and under your control.

Regular updates bring not just bug fixes, but meaningful feature enhancements based on real user feedback. The development team actively listens to the community and implements requested improvements, ensuring the software evolves alongside your needs.

The application seamlessly integrates with your existing workflow, supporting drag-and-drop functionality, Quick Look previews, Spotlight searching, and even Shortcuts automation for power users.
